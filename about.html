About Tech 101

Empowering Independence Through Technology

Our Program

Tech 101 Foundation provides comprehensive technology education and hands-on opportunities in Palm Beach County. We offer workshops, classes, and personal coaching designed to help you master your devices.


Our team acts as a community partner. We provide the technical expertise and a patient learning environment, allowing you to focus on the skills you want to build.

How We Work

  • Affirmative: Our curriculum focuses on practical, real-world tasks and successful outcomes.
  • Accessible: We design our materials and instruction to be clear, readable, and easy to follow.
  • Dignified: We offer a professional partnership centered on your specific goals and questions.

Join Our Community

We partner with you to build on your skills and interests. You gain more knowledge to connect digitally with loved ones and protect your personal information.
